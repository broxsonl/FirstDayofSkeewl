About the Game

‘First Day of Skeewl’ is a ‘Choose Your Own Adventure’-style game where you, the player, goes through the first day of middle school. The player will be presented with several questions as the game progresses. Some of these choices may result in earning points or losing points. Some may result in losing the game entirely.
